Morondava, Madagascar

Morondava is a coastal town in Madagascar known for being a gateway to the iconic Avenue of the Baobabs. The area offers a mix of natural attractions, including unique wildlife in Kirindy Reserve, the natural beauty of the Mangroves of Belo-sur-Mer, and beautiful beaches. Visitors can enjoy a relaxed seaside atmosphere, local culture, and activities like boat trips and wildlife spotting.
