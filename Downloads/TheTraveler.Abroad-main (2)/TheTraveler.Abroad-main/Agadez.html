Agadez, Niger

Agadez or Agadès (in tifinagh: ⴰⴶⴰⴷⴰⵣ) is the most important place in Nord Niger, located in the Sahara and Sahel. The name Agadez comes back to the site that you can “return to visit” at the same time. In effect, the ville has the carrefour of caravans that are connected to North Africa to the Africa subsaharienne. This is true, it is a commercial centre.
