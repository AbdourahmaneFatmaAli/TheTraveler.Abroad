About Us

The Traveler.Abroad

Dream Destination across Africa


TheTraveler.Abroad is your guide to explore Africa. We believe that travel is more than just visiting places its about experiencing culture, the people and the stories that make each destination unique.


We want every traveler to feel inspired. On this site, you can read real stories from people who have explored Africa, see beautiful pictures. Whether you are looking for adventure, or just some travel inspiration, TheTraveler.Abroad has something for you.


Our community makes the website special. Visitors can become contributors by sharing their own stories and photos.


We also provide helpful links to book accommodations, tours and activities so that your journey goes from planning to experiencing.
